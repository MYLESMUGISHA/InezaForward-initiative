RefugeeGlobalTech

Born from the belief that kindness and technology can transform lives

Our Story

RefugeeGlobalTech was founded in 2025 as part of the RiseWithThem initiative, recognizing that refugees bring incredible talent, resilience, and perspective to the technology sector. Our name comes from "Ineza," meaning "good" in Kinyarwanda, reflecting our core belief in the inherent goodness of people helping people.

Our Approach

We believe that sustainable change requires addressing root causes, not just symptoms. Our 15-month program combines intensive technical training, language support, mental health services, career coaching, industry connections, and wraparound services for families.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've graduated over 200 refugees into tech careers, with an 85% job placement rate and average starting salaries of $65,000. We've restored professional identities, reunited families, and created a network of tech professionals giving back to their communities.
